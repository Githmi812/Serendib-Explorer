Who We Are

Born from a Love for
The Pearl of the Indian Ocean

Serendib Explorer was founded by a group of Sri Lankan travel enthusiasts who believed that the island's greatest treasures were not in tourist brochures — they were hidden in mist-covered hill country roads, ancient temple courtyards at dawn, and conversations with village elders over Ceylon tea.

"We don't just plan trips. We craft memories that last a lifetime — rooted in the genuine spirit of Sri Lanka."

From the sun-drenched beaches of the South to the sacred plateau of Horton Plains, from the colonial grandeur of Galle Fort to the timeless serenity of Anuradhapura — every journey we design carries the fingerprint of local knowledge and heartfelt passion.
