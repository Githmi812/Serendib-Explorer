Popular Sri Lankan Destinations

Handpicked journeys through Ceylon's finest treasures

Western Province

The Western Province is the commercial and urban hub of Sri Lanka. Tourism here centers around Colombo’s city attractions, shopping malls, museums, nightlife, and nearby beaches like Negombo and Mount Lavinia. It blends modern life with colonial history.

Discover More
Central Province

This province is famous for its cool climate, scenic hills, and cultural heritage. Key attractions include Kandy (Temple of the Tooth Relic), tea plantations, waterfalls, and nature spots like Horton Plains. It is a major cultural and eco-tourism region.

Discover More
Southern Province

The Southern Province is well known for beautiful beaches and historic sites. Tourists visit Galle Fort, Mirissa, Unawatuna, and Hikkaduwa for beach tourism, whale watching, and colonial architecture.

Discover More
Northern Province

Tourism in the Northern Province highlights Tamil culture, Hindu temples, and historic sites. Jaffna, Nallur Kandaswamy Temple, islands like Delft, and quiet beaches attract visitors interested in culture and heritage.

Discover More
Eastern Province

The Eastern Province is popular for its unspoiled beaches and marine tourism. Destinations such as Nilaveli, Pasikudah, and Arugam Bay are famous for swimming, diving, and surfing. It also has rich cultural diversity.

Discover More
North Western Province

This province offers a mix of religious, historical, and coastal tourism. Wilpattu National Park, Munneswaram Temple, and fishing villages attract nature lovers and pilgrims. It is less crowded and peaceful.

Discover More
North Central Province

The North Central Province is the heart of ancient Sri Lankan civilization. Major attractions include Anuradhapura and Polonnaruwa, which are UNESCO World Heritage Sites. Tourism focuses on history, archaeology, and religious pilgrimages.

Discover More
Uva Province

Uva Province is known for its natural beauty and adventure tourism. Ella, Badulla, waterfalls, and hiking trails draw tourists who enjoy mountains, rail journeys, and eco-tourism experiences.

Discover More
Sabaragamuwa Province

This province is famous for gem mining, rainforests, and religious tourism. Key attractions include Adam’s Peak (Sri Pada), Sinharaja Rainforest, and scenic landscapes. It attracts pilgrims and nature enthusiasts alike.
